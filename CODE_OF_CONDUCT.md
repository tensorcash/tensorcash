# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the TensorCash project a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We are building
financial infrastructure in the open; that demands rigour, candour, and respect
in equal measure.

## Standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Focusing on technical merit; backing claims with code, tests, or proofs.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive review (see the ACK/NACK
  convention in `CONTRIBUTING.md` — a NACK is about the change, not the person).
- Showing empathy and patience toward other contributors.

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment of any kind, personal attacks,
publishing others' private information, and sustained disruption of discussion
or review.

## Scope

This code applies within all project spaces — repositories, issues, pull
requests, and any official communication channel — and when an individual is
representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behaviour to `conduct@tensorcash.org`. Reports are handled
confidentially. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions, and may
temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for behaviour they deem
inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
